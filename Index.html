About Me

I'm a Business & Data Analyst with a background in Industrial Engineering and a passion for transforming raw data into meaningful insights. I specialize in building reporting systems, automating dashboards, and helping organizations make informed decisions through tools like Looker Studio, Power BI, Tableau, SQL, and LookML.

Currently working in the financial industry, I led the implementation of an end-to-end analytics ecosystem for the Innovation area, scaling it from ad-hoc reports to an automated suite used by cross-functional teams. I've developed dashboards for stakeholders in fraud, sales, planning, and operations—always focusing on impact, usability, and scalability.

Earlier in my career, I took on a pioneering role at a mid-sized company to bring data visibility into operational decisions. That experience strengthened my analytical thinking, communication with stakeholders, and hands-on experience with data tools. I believe great analysis goes beyond numbers—it solves real business problems.

Outside of work, I'm a father of two, a lifelong learner currently improving my English and French, and a passionate guitarist and football fan. I value curiosity, empathy, and building things that last.
