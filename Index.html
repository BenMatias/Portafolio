About Me

I currently work as a Business & Data Analyst in the financial industry, focused on dashboard development, metric tracking, and reporting that supports operational and strategic decision-making. My work includes implementing reporting systems from scratch, as well as exploratory analysis and automation using tools like Looker Studio, Power BI, SQL, and Tableau.

I hold a degree in Industrial Civil Engineering and entered the world of analytics after identifying major gaps in performance visibility at my organization—gaps that I was determined to close. Since then, I’ve grown passionate about transforming raw data into actionable insights that make a tangible impact.

Before working in data, I held roles in business operations and quickly gravitated toward data-focused responsibilities, realizing the power of data storytelling to guide strategy and improve outcomes. I’ve since led high-impact analytics projects while continuing to develop my technical and leadership skills.

Outside of work, I’m raising two young children, learning English and French, and exploring ways to integrate analytics into everyday life. I’m passionate about music and play guitar, as well as enjoy drawing, football, tennis, running, and video games. I believe in lifelong learning, sharing knowledge, and collaborating on meaningful, data-driven solutions.
